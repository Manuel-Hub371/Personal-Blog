Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

Welcome to Manuel-HUB. Your privacy is paramount, and this policy explains transparently how I collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with Google AdSense and global privacy standards.

This website uses cookies to serve personalized advertisements and analyze site traffic.

Our site contains links to external platforms (WhatsApp, GitHub, LinkedIn). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their specific privacy statements.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have certain data protection rights, including the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Page or email us directly at manuel@manuel-hub.com.
