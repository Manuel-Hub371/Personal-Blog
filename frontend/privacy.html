Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Manuel-HUB ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the website or otherwise when you contact us.

  • Name and Contact Data (email address).
  • Credentials (passwords, hints, and similar security information used for authentication).

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many other websites, we use "cookies" to enhance your experience. A cookie is a tiny file that is stored on your computer. Cookies are used for keeping track of settings (such as your preferred theme) and analyzing site traffic.

We may specifically use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this website and other websites on the internet. You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To send you administrative information.
  • To protect our Services (ensuring security and preventing fraud).
  • To respond to user inquiries/offer support to users.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region (such as the EEA or California), you have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. These may include the right (i) to request access and obtain a copy of your personal information, (ii) to request rectification or erasure; (iii) to restrict the processing of your personal information; and (iv) if applicable, to data portability.
